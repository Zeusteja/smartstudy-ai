1. Newton’s First Law: An object in motion stays in motion unless acted upon.
2. Newton’s Second Law: F = ma
3. Newton’s Third Law: Every action has an equal and opposite reaction.
4. Acceleration = Change in velocity / Time
5. Speed = Distance / Time
6. Velocity = Displacement / Time
7. Gravitational Force: F = G(m₁m₂)/r²
8. Work = Force × Distance
9. Power = Work / Time
10. Kinetic Energy = ½mv²
11. Potential Energy = mgh
12. Conservation of Energy: Total energy remains constant.
13. Ohm’s Law: V = IR
14. Resistance: R = ρL/A
15. Current: I = Q/t
16. Electric Power: P = VI
17. Capacitance: C = Q/V
18. Magnetic force: F = qvBsinθ
19. Snell’s Law: n₁sinθ₁ = n₂sinθ₂
20. Refractive index = Speed in vacuum / Speed in medium
21. Frequency = 1 / Time period
22. Wavelength = Speed / Frequency
23. Momentum: p = mv
24. Impulse = Change in momentum
25. Hooke's Law: F = -kx
26. Sound travels faster in solids than in liquids or gases.
27. Thermodynamics: ΔU = Q - W
28. Ideal Gas Law: PV = nRT
29. Heat = mass × specific heat × ΔT
30. Light is both a wave and a particle (wave-particle duality).
